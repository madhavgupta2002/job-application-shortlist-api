JOB APPLICATION SHORTLISTING





ABOUT

This is a web tool that uses AI to detect the tone of a job application and determine whether it is a formal application or not. By analyzing various linguistic features such as sentence structure, word choice, and grammar, the tool can accurately identify the tone of an email and provide useful insights to recruiters and HR personnel. This NLP technology has the potential to streamline the hiring process and help enterprises quickly identify the most qualified candidates. With its ability to efficiently sort through job applications, the tool can save valuable time and resources while making the hiring process more effective.
The Tone Detection Web Tool is an innovative solution for enterprises seeking to streamline the hiring process and identify the most qualified candidates. This tool uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze the tone and communication style of job applications and identify whether an email is a formal job application or not.
